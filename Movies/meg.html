"The Meg" is a thrilling science fiction action film that revolves around a group of scientists who face off against a colossal prehistoric shark known as the Megalodon. Starring Jason Statham, the movie is filled with breathtaking underwater action, suspense, and a battle for survival against the giant predator. "The Meg" offers an exhilarating adventure for fans of monster films.
